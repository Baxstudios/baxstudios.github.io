6 Reasons why you need Animation

Drive social engagement

Animated visuals and videos are trendy on social media. Lovely animated characters can be powerful attention-grabbers in advertising. They are memorable, attractive, funny, and often drive specific emotions and feelings. We associate favorite characters with brands that stay behind them. They catch an eye, make us interested in a brand, and in the products/services it offers.

Convey Complex Ideas

In storytelling, the number one rule is to show not tell. Animation helps you transit complex ideas in a simple form. Do you need to promote a product or service that is excellent in its concept, but it’s difficult to explain to the audience? Animation advertising may be your perfect solution.

Create a lasting impression:

By using animation in advertisement, you will make your brand stand out from other companies and attract the audience’s attention. Showcase that you are thinking out of the box. Convey an idea of the exclusiveness and uniqueness of your approaches as well as your products and services. It will help your brand build an unforgettable image and set you apart from the competitors.

Boost conversion rates

Using animation to advertise a product can boost conversion rates by simplifying the message and engaging viewers. 64% of website visitors are more likely to buy a product on an online retail site after watching a video. In addition, visitors who view videos stay on the site an average of 2 minutes longer than those who don’t view videos.

Reach a Broader Audience:

All of us are like kids; we love cartoons from childhood. An engaging cartoony animation can attract both children and adults. Due to its versatility and creativity, it can be relevant to different target groups. Animation in ads has been used since the very beginning of advertising itself and it does not lose its relevance today. Use funny and beautiful animation to engage more people and make your advert reach a broader audience.

Boost your S.E.O

Having animated videos on your business’ website shows search engines that your site is enriched with great media content. Consumers are demanding more and more video content from their online usage, which means that search engines will rank your website higher when it features an animated video.
