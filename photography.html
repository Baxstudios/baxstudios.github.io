6 Reasons why you need Photography

Drive Sales

There is a direct link between utilizing high quality images in marketing and the growth of sales. Photographs help consumers to visualize themselves using a product or service and allows them to imagine it improving their life in some way.

Gets attention

With the sheer volume of information being shared online, 1.5 billion units of content – Our team does everything they can to grab the attention of your target audience. Visual elements increase people’s willingness to read content by a staggering 80%.

Non-Verbal Communication

With 93% of all human communication being non-verbal, combining visual elements with well-written text is an excellent way for your brand to capture the imagination of your target audience.

Viral Option

Visual content can go viral if an audience likes it well enough to share it with their online communities and social networks. This helps to build brand credibility, corporate integrity and customer loyalty.

Influences Emotion

In spite of efforts to make purchases based on rational analysis, consumers often let emotion influence their buying decisions. The use of captivating photographs drives emotional responses about a brand.

Social Engagement

Photographs are easier to consume than text. Facebook campaigns generate 65% more engagement and tweets average 35% more retweets when including a photograph than those without.
